Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

1. Who we are

“Stavki s golovoy” is a football analytics content channel operated by an individual creator (the “Operator”). Contact: info@stavkisgolovoy.com.

2. What data we process

This website is a static informational site. It does not require registration, does not set its own cookies and does not collect personal data from visitors.

Our application integrates with the TikTok API (Login Kit and Content Posting API) solely to publish the Operator's own content to the Operator's own TikTok account. Through this integration we process only: (a) OAuth access tokens for the Operator's own account, and (b) basic account information returned by the TikTok API (such as account ID and display name). We do not collect, store or process personal data of any other TikTok users.

3. How data is used

Access tokens are used exclusively to authenticate API requests for publishing content and checking publishing status. They are stored securely by our authentication provider and are never sold or shared with third parties.

4. Data retention and deletion

Access tokens are retained only while the integration is active and can be revoked at any time from the TikTok account security settings or by contacting us. Upon revocation, tokens become invalid.

5. Third-party services

Content is published to platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, X) that process data under their own privacy policies. This website is hosted on Vercel; the hosting provider may log standard technical data (such as IP addresses) as described in its privacy statement.

6. Your rights

If you believe we hold any personal data about you (EU/EEA visitors: under the GDPR), you may request access, correction or deletion by writing to info@stavkisgolovoy.com.

7. Changes

This policy may be updated; the current version is always available on this page.
